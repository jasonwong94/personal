Summer 1T4:

Summer Research Volunter

I will be working under Professor W.T. Ng under the VLSI Research Group. I will be under the supervision of PhD Candidate Andrew Shorten. The research is determining the relationship between the gate current (ig) and collector current (ic) of IGBTs (insulated gate bipolar transistor- a hybrid between BJTs and MOSFETs) that can be as 'live' feedback in IGBT applications. IGBTs are currently used in DC-DC converters and require external components to 'stabalize' the current to mitigate power loss.

From this experience, my goal is to:

  1. Become more familiar with electronic datasheets for components
  2. Become fluent and familiar with circuit simulation programs (HSpice and Altium)
  3. Understand PCB (printed circuit board) design conventions and rules
  4. Understand the practical limitations of transistors
